What is/

BarCamp?

BarCamp is an international network of user-generated conferences primarily focused on technology and the web. They are open, participatory workshop-events, the content of which is provided by participants.

The key difference between BarCamp and traditional conferences is participation. There is no audience, only participants. Attendees must give a demo, a session, or help with one, or otherwise volunteer / contribute in some way to support the event. All presentation topics are posted on our official website to be voted. The topics voted the most will be presented on Barcamp day.

Barcamps are typically free to attend and the speakers are the attendees themselves. That means anybody can be a presenter . Anyone who feels he or she is able to contribute is welcome.

Barcamps support the innovative spirit and sharing of knowledge. It’s an opportunity for people to get together to network and create new business ideas or new products and services. It’s a place to build strong community .

FAQs

Who is BarCamp for ?  

Anyone who is tired of typical conferences filled by boring presentations or sales pitches, really. If you want to experience the real spirit of sharing and learning, BarCamp is for you!

Is an invite or registration required? 

No, but it's very helpful for us organizers to plan ahead! So please take a minute to register for the event and spread the word for your friends to do the same. Walk-ins are welcome as long as we’re still within venue capacity.

Are there any rules? 

Read the Official Rules of BarCamp, or check out this Unofficial FAQ in VietNam. We celebrate sharing and openness, but BarCamp is not a commercial or promotional platform, so please refrain from pitching your service/organization and deviating from the sharing and learning experience. We also follow the Law of Two Feet.

What should I do if I'd like to host a session?

Come to BarCamp early as we take topic submissions and put together the schedule in the morning. You might also want to post your topic on our Facebook Wall in advance as we provide priority scheduling for topics that receive a lot of Likes. Be sure to read and follow BarCamp rules (see above).

Is BarCamp Da Nang in English only? 

No, Cantonese and Mandarin are most certainly welcome as well. We ask for the spoken language(s) with each topic submission and have the language(s) indicated on the schedule wall.

What should I bring to BarCamp?

Please have a copy of your Eventbrite ticket with you for sign-in (soft copy is fine). If you plan to present or show a demo, bring your own laptop, charger and VGA adapter. And of course, don’t forget your business cards if you’re into networking.

Will there be T-shirts for sale or for grabs? 

Yes! You can buy the T-shirts while you register and pick up on the day at the sign-in booth.

WHY Barcamp Danang?

Unlike traditional seminars where gather a group of people talking about a topic.
Barcamp possesses much more interesting stories to tell.

Place to nurture relationships

Passion, idea, enthusiasm and curiosity motivate people from all walks of life to join Barcamp, making it second to no one for the variety of attendees; thus, there is no doubt to confirm that Barcamp is the best place for building great relationships.

Exhibition of breaking ideas

Barcamp is well known for wonderful ideas brought by a number of attendees to a place where they are discussed, explored, developed, implemented and even financed if good enough.

Starting point of potential companies

Barcamp really attracts startupers all over the world. Thanks to its openness and, in turn, gathering of various attendees, Barcamp brings suitable partners together in the same place with investors; thus, wonderful startup companies are born.
